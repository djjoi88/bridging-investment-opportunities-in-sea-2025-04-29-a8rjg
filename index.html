Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment requirement?

Our investment opportunities typically start from $50,000 USD, with some premium deals requiring higher minimums. We offer flexible investment structures to accommodate different investor profiles.

How do you select investment opportunities?

Our rigorous selection process involves comprehensive due diligence, market analysis, and team assessment. Less than 5% of reviewed opportunities make it to our platform.

What regions do you cover?

We focus on Southeast Asian markets, including Singapore, Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, and the Philippines, with particular emphasis on high-growth sectors like technology, healthcare, and consumer markets.

How long does the investment process take?

The typical investment process takes 4-8 weeks from initial interest to deal closure, depending on the complexity and size of the investment.

What support do you provide post-investment?

We offer ongoing portfolio monitoring, quarterly performance reports, and access to our network of industry experts. Our team provides regular updates and assists with any corporate actions or exit opportunities.
